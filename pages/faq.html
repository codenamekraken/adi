What is an STL file?

STL files store information about three-dimensional objects. Specifically, the surface geometry of an object with no representation of color, texture, etc.

How can I print this STL file?

You can purchase a 3D printer and print it yourself, or use a service like Shapeways. There you can upload your model and pay to have it printed in various materials like plastic, or even metal.
